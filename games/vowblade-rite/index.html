How to play Vowblade Rite

Touch or click, drag to aim a slash route, then release. Your knight order lunges along that line and cleaves every foe hugging the path. Finish each wave and choose one of three vows to shape the next fight.

  • Drag anywhere on the arena to preview the slash line from your knight.
  • Release to dash. A wider, longer line hits more enemies.
  • Kill several enemies in one stroke to trigger a Flourish burst.
  • Between waves, pick a vow to gain range, echoes, wards, trails, or stronger Flourish bonuses.
