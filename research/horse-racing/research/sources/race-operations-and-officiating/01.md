## Snippet

The Judge has the primary function of determining the finishing order of the horses at the end of the race, using the photofinish as an aid. The Clerk of the Scales' primary task is the weighing out and weighing in of riders before and after the race and thereby ensuring horses carry the weight allocated to them in the race.
