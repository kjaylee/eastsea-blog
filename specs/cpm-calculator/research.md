# CPM Calculator — Research

## What
CPM = Cost Per Mille (cost per 1,000 impressions). Universal advertising metric.

## Search Intent
- "cpm calculator" — high volume, evergreen, advertisers + publishers
- Users want to solve for any one of: CPM, Cost, or Impressions given the other two
- Secondary: compare CPM across campaigns, estimate budget needs

## Formula
- CPM = (Total Cost / Impressions) × 1,000
- Cost = (CPM × Impressions) / 1,000
- Impressions = (Total Cost / CPM) × 1,000

## Competitive Landscape
- calculatorsoup, omnicalculator, clickz all have basic CPM calcs
- Differentiation: solve for any variable, dark premium UI, bilingual ko/en, ad budget planner table
