# Research: Dropshipping Profit Margin Calculator

## Intent
"dropshipping profit calculator", "dropshipping margin calculator" — high commercial intent, targets ecommerce entrepreneurs evaluating dropshipping viability.

## Existing catalog check
- No "dropshipping" tool in 614-tool catalog.
- Related but distinct: amazon-fba-profit-calculator (FBA-specific), marketplace-fee-profit-calculator (generic marketplace), ecommerce-contribution-margin-stack-calculator (DTC-focused).

## Key inputs for dropshipping
1. **Product cost** (supplier price from AliExpress/CJ Dropshipping etc.)
2. **Selling price** (store price)
3. **Shipping cost** (ePacket, supplier shipping, or 3PL)
4. **Platform fee %** (Shopify plan cost per order, or marketplace fee)
5. **Payment processing fee** (Stripe/PayPal %, flat fee)
6. **Ad spend per order** (Facebook/Google CPA)
7. **Returns rate %** (with refund cost)
8. **Monthly fixed costs** (Shopify subscription, apps, domains)
9. **Orders per month** (to amortize fixed costs)

## Key outputs
- Revenue per order
- Total cost per order (product + shipping + fees + ad CPA + amortized fixed)
- Net profit per order
- Net margin %
- Monthly net profit
- Break-even orders/month
- Break-even selling price
- ROI on ad spend (ROAS)

## Differentiation
This is NOT a generic fee calculator. It specifically models the dropshipping cost structure: supplier cost, high ad spend per order, returns, and amortized fixed costs — the core decision tool for dropshipping profitability.
