## Search Intent Analysis

- **Buyer intent**: HR managers evaluating retention programs, business owners budgeting for recruitment, consultants building business cases for retention initiatives
- **Monetization**: HR software affiliates (BambooHR, Workday, Gusto), ATS tools, recruitment agency ads

## Industry Benchmarks
- SHRM estimates average cost-per-hire: ~$4,700 (2024)
- Gallup: replacing an employee costs 50-200% of annual salary
- Entry-level: ~50% of salary; Mid-level: ~125%; Senior/C-suite: up to 200%+
- Average time-to-fill: 36-42 days
- New hire ramp-up to full productivity: 3-8 months

## Cost Categories (Standard Framework)
1. **Separation costs**: Exit interview time, administrative processing, severance
2. **Recruitment costs**: Job postings, recruiter fees (15-25% of salary), advertising, referral bonuses
3. **Selection costs**: Resume screening, interviewing (interviewer hours × hourly rate × # interviews), background checks, assessments
4. **Onboarding & training**: Orientation, training materials, mentor time, formal training programs
5. **Lost productivity - Vacancy**: Revenue/output lost during unfilled period (vacancy days × daily output value)
6. **Lost productivity - Ramp-up**: New hire operates at reduced capacity for months (typically modeled as 25%→50%→75%→100% over months)
7. **Morale/knowledge loss**: Hard to quantify but real — institutional knowledge, team disruption
