# FIRE Calculator — Research

## What is FIRE?
Financial Independence, Retire Early — a movement focused on extreme savings and investment to retire far earlier than traditional budgets allow.

## Key Inputs
1. Current age
2. Target retirement age (or "FIRE age")
3. Current savings/investments
4. Annual income (after tax)
5. Annual expenses
6. Expected annual return on investments (%)
7. Expected inflation rate (%)
8. Safe withdrawal rate (default 4% — the "4% rule")
9. Monthly savings contribution

## Key Outputs
1. **FIRE Number** — the portfolio size needed (Annual Expenses / SWR)
2. **Years to FIRE** — how many years until you reach your FIRE number
3. **FIRE Age** — current age + years to FIRE
4. **Monthly savings needed** — if targeting a specific FIRE age
5. **Savings rate** — % of income saved
6. **Year-by-year projection table** — portfolio growth over time
7. **Coast FIRE number** — amount needed now so compound growth alone reaches FIRE number by target age
8. **Lean FIRE / Fat FIRE** variants (different expense levels)

## Search Volume / ROI Justification
- "FIRE calculator" — high search volume, evergreen

## Differentiation from existing tools
- `retirement-savings-calculator` exists but is traditional retirement focused
- FIRE calculator specifically addresses the FIRE movement methodology (4% rule, savings rate, Coast FIRE, Lean/Fat FIRE)
