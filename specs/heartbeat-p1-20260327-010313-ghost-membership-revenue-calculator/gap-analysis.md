# Gap Analysis

## What Already Exists
- Platform fee calculators for Patreon, Substack, Ko-fi, Gumroad, Payhip, Kajabi, Podia, Lemon Squeezy, Memberful, Sellfy, and more.
- Broader creator monetization tools for newsletters, sponsorships, memberships, and paid upgrades.

## What Was Missing
- No Ghost-specific monetization calculator.
- No Ghost plan-cost-aware recurring-membership take-home tool.
- No exact-match Ghost revenue slug on any discovery surface.

## Why This Gap Matters
- Ghost is a common direct-membership destination for blogs and newsletters.
- Searchers with Ghost intent are poorly served by generic membership calculators.
- A direct Ghost slug is more discoverable than asking users to infer Ghost economics from unrelated tools.
