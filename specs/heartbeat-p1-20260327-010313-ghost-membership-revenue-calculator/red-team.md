# Red Team

## Overlap Risk
- Risk: tool feels like another generic creator membership calculator.
- Mitigation: keep framing explicitly Ghost-specific, direct-Stripe, and plan-cost-aware.

## Stale Pricing Risk
- Risk: hard-coded Ghost pricing becomes outdated.
- Mitigation: do not ship fixed Ghost plan tables or claims about current official plan tiers; use editable plan cost input.

## Formula Confusion Risk
- Risk: annual memberships could be mistaken for same-month cash collection.
- Mitigation: label the model as monthly-equivalent planning and repeat that annual memberships are spread across 12 months.

## Low-Signal Output Risk
- Risk: outputs duplicate existing fee calculators.
- Mitigation: include Ghost-plan break-even and target-net member requirement at the current monthly/annual mix.

## UX Risk
- Risk: bilingual copy becomes too dense.
- Mitigation: keep labels short, use a single note for model caveats, and emphasize the first-screen KPI row.
