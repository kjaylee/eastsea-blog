# Gap Analysis — Beacons Fee Calculator

## Why this fills a gap
- The repo has multiple creator-fee tools, but no Beacons-specific page.
- Search intent is platform-exact rather than generic.
- The tool answers a concrete upgrade decision:
  - stay on 9% seller-fee plan
  - move to a 0% seller-fee plan

## Overlap assessment
- Low overlap with `ko-fi-fee-calculator`, `buy-me-a-coffee-fee-calculator`, and `memberful-fee-calculator` because Beacons mixes link-in-bio, store, affiliates, and plan-based seller fees.
- Lower overlap than adding another generic profit calculator for subscriptions or marketplaces.

## Remaining gaps after this ship
- A future Beacons-specific creator stack comparator could compare Beacons vs Stan Store vs Gumroad.
- A future Beacons affiliate link payout estimator could focus on affiliate-only monetization rather than store product sales.
