# Gap Analysis: `kdp-royalty-calculator`

## Current repo coverage
- Strong on creator platforms: Patreon, Ko-fi, Buy Me a Coffee, Gumroad, Substack, beehiiv comparisons.
- Strong on marketplace/seller fees: Etsy, eBay, Depop, Mercari, Whatnot, Amazon FBA, Amazon Handmade.
- Strong on generic monetization levers: paywalls, pricing, add-ons, upsells, ads, sponsorships.

## Missing surface
- No exact-match KDP calculator.
- No author-publishing royalty tool.
- No tool combining eBook sale royalties with KU page-read earnings.

## Why overlap stays low
- Audience is authors/publishers, not sellers or newsletter creators.
- Revenue model is royalty-plan math, not marketplace fee take-rate math.
- KDP Select pages-read income is unique to this platform.
