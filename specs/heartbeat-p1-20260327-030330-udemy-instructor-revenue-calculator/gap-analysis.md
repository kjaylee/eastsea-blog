# Gap Analysis

## Why This Is Not Redundant

- `teachable-fee-calculator`, `kajabi-fee-calculator`, `podia-fee-calculator`, and `sellfy-pricing-calculator` focus on direct-seller plan fees and payment processing.
- `patreon-net-revenue-calculator` and `onlyfans-earnings-calculator` focus on creator subscriptions and tips.
- `udemy-instructor-revenue-calculator` is different because the dominant mental model is revenue-share by acquisition channel plus a separate engagement-based subscription pool.

## Residual Gap After Ship

- The tool will still use an editable assumption for partner / external-affiliate share because the official public docs used here do not cleanly publish that rate alongside the 97% / 37% rules.
- Subscription scenarios remain estimate-driven, which is appropriate for pre-launch planning but not a substitute for the Udemy revenue report.
