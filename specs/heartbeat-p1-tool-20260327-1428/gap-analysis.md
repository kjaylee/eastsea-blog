# Gap Analysis — Fractional CMO Pricing Calculator

## Remaining minor gaps
1. The tool is USD-only.
   - Why acceptable now: search intent is global enough, but USD is the cleanest first shipping slice for proposal math.
2. It does not include niche multipliers (B2B SaaS vs ecommerce vs PLG vs enterprise).
   - Why acceptable now: users can still model this through hours and rates; niche presets can be a future expansion.
3. No local persistence for draft quotes.
   - Why acceptable now: copy-ready summary solves the immediate proposal workflow without adding complexity.

## Risk accepted
- The calculator is heuristic, not a market benchmark database.
- Mitigation already present in UI: explicit disclaimer + FAQ framing.

## Next sensible iteration (not required for this shipment)
- Add presets for startup, Series A SaaS, ecommerce, and marketplace retention models.
