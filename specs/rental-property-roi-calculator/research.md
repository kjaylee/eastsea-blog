- "rental property calculator" — high volume, commercial intent
- "cash on cash return calculator" — investor keyword
- "cap rate calculator" — real estate investor staple
- "rental property ROI" — direct match
- "investment property calculator" — broad match

## Not in catalog
- Confirmed: no rental/property/real-estate/landlord/cap-rate/noi/cash-on-cash tools exist

## Tool Concept
All-in-one rental property investment calculator covering:
1. **Purchase Details**: price, down payment %, closing costs
2. **Mortgage**: loan amount, interest rate, term
3. **Income**: monthly rent, other income
4. **Expenses**: property tax, insurance, maintenance, vacancy rate, property management fee, HOA
5. **Outputs**: Monthly cash flow, Annual NOI, Cap Rate, Cash-on-Cash Return, Total ROI, Monthly mortgage payment

## Competitive Edge
- Most free calculators only cover 1-2 metrics
- Ours covers all key metrics in one page
- Clean UI with instant calculation
- SEO-friendly for multiple keywords
