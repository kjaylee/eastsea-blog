# Research: Retirement Savings Calculator

## Search Volume & Commercial Viability
- "retirement calculator" — ~1.5M monthly searches (US)
- "retirement savings calculator" — ~200K monthly
- "how much to save for retirement" — ~100K monthly
- High ad CPC ($5-15) — finance niche, excellent AdSense RPM
- Competitors: NerdWallet, Bankrate, Fidelity — but all require complex UIs/accounts
- Static single-page calculator can rank on long-tail: "simple retirement calculator", "retirement savings goal calculator"

## Gap in Catalog
- No retirement-related tool exists in 625+ tool catalog
- Adjacent tools: compound-interest-calculator, savings-goal-simulator, burn-rate-calculator
- Clear differentiation: retirement-specific inputs (current age, retirement age, life expectancy, Social Security, inflation-adjusted)

## Core Features for MVP
1. Current age, retirement age, life expectancy
2. Current savings, monthly contribution
3. Expected annual return (pre/post retirement)
4. Inflation rate
5. Desired monthly retirement income
6. Output: projected savings at retirement, monthly withdrawal sustainable, savings gap/surplus, year-by-year projection chart

## Design Pattern
- Follow existing dark-theme card layout (burn-rate-calculator pattern)
- Bilingual KO/EN labels
- analytics.js included
- Mobile-responsive grid
