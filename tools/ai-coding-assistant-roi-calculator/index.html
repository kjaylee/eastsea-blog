💡 How We Calculate ROI

📐 Formula

Monthly Savings = (Coding hrs/mo × Productivity %) × Devs × Hourly Rate

Annual ROI = (Annual Savings − Annual Cost) / Annual Cost × 100

📊 Productivity Data

Gain estimates are based on GitHub's 2023 research (Copilot 26–55%), McKinsey's AI developer study, and vendor benchmarks. Conservative values are used.
