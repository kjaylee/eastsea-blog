Copy-ready summary

  • Official public doc baseline: Amazon Handmade deducts 15% or $0.30 minimum, whichever is greater, for each unit shipped.
  • This model uses the buyer charge basis of product price + shipping charged to buyer + gift-wrap charged to buyer.
  • Taxes collected by Amazon are excluded. Returns, refunds, and special regional fees are intentionally out of scope for this first slice.
