FAQ

What does the 70% vs 85% proceeds split mean?

Use the 70% bucket for standard App Store proceeds and the 85% bucket for subscriptions or programs that qualify for reduced commission economics in your current reporting month.

Should annual subscribers be total active annual subscribers?

No. Enter annual subscriptions that were actually billed this month so the payout estimate matches monthly cash collection more closely.

Does this include VAT, GST, and refunds?

Yes. The calculator removes VAT or GST first, then applies the refund rate before calculating App Store payout and take-home.

How to use this payout estimate

  • Use current-month billed counts, not lifetime installs.
  • Keep the 70% and 85% buckets separate to see real proceeds uplift.
  • Compare payout against active-subscriber support cost and fixed monthly spend before increasing acquisition budget.
