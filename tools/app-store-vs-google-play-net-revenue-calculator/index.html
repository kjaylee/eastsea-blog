Formula notes

When to use this page

Use this tool when you are comparing identical customer spend across stores for paid apps or one-time digital sales. If your real question is recurring billing or web checkout capture, switch to the matching comparator instead of stretching this one beyond scope.

Why is there a separate subscription comparator?

Subscription proceeds have different Apple and Google rules than one-time digital sales. Mixing them here would make the page slower, noisier, and easier to misunderstand.

What does Google YTD earnings mean here?

It represents the revenue already counted toward Google Play’s first-$1M 15% tier. The calculator then decides how much of the current scenario still fits inside the discounted bucket.

Are taxes, FX, or alternative billing included?

No. VAT/GST is handled only as a simple rate removal. FX conversion, alternative billing programs, territory-specific exceptions, and tax filing logic are intentionally excluded from v1.

Can I copy the output into a memo or launch deck?

Yes. The summary box is built for quick sharing in docs, notes, or internal launch planning. Just keep the assumptions with the output so others do not confuse it with actual settlement data.
