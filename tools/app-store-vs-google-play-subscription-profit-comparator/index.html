All Tools Apple Proceeds Tool
This is a subscription fee comparator, not a full accounting tool. Taxes or VAT, territory-specific rules, family plans, and alternative billing programs are excluded in v1. Apple subscription proceeds change with Small Business status and the share of subscriber-months beyond year one. Google Play is modeled with the official 15% subscription fee baseline.
