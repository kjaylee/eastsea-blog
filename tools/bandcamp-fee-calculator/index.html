Formula notes
  • Bandcamp fee basis = min(sale amount, $100) for digital items in this v1.
  • Bandcamp revenue share = fee basis × current tier rate (15% or 10%).
  • Processor fee uses the under-$8.07 alternate rate of 5% + $0.05; otherwise it uses the selected payment-method preset.
  • Target price is solved numerically against net after fees and seller cost.
