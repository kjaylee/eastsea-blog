Buy Me a Coffee Fee Calculator

Model real creator take-home after the 5% platform fee, Stripe 2.9% + $0.30 card processing, 0.5% payout drag, refunds, reward costs, and fixed monthly overhead.

Defaults reflect public Buy Me a Coffee documentation and can change. Use this as a planning model, not a billing statement.

Assumptions & interpretation

  • Default baseline: 5% platform fee, 2.9% + $0.30 card processing, 0.5% payout drag.
  • Refunds reduce kept volume before payout and operating-cost math.
  • When supporters cover card fees, this tool reverse-prices the supporter charge so the creator still receives the intended support amount after processing.
  • Break-even math holds the current monthly transaction count constant and solves for the average support amount required.
