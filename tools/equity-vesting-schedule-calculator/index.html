How this works

The calculator assumes linear vesting. At the cliff event, it releases the cumulative portion earned through the cliff period. After that, vesting continues at the selected cadence until the full grant is vested.

If a start date lands on the 29th, 30th, or 31st, future event dates use the last valid day of the month when needed. When the term is not evenly divisible by the cadence, the final event absorbs the remainder so the total stays exact.
