FAQ

Why does the shipped fee use 10% and a $0.80 minimum?

That is the public baseline this page is built around because multiple 2025–2026 sources describe shipped Marketplace orders that way after the old 5% model. If your account or region differs, use the custom override rather than trusting the default.

Does Facebook charge a fee for local pickup?

This calculator assumes local pickup has a 0% Marketplace fee baseline. It also removes buyer-paid shipping revenue and shipping-label cost, so you can see whether local pickup is actually more profitable for the same item.

Does this include taxes, disputes, or ad attribution complexity?

No. This is a planning model for seller take-home, not an accounting system. Add packaging, ad spend, and other seller costs here, but treat taxes and policy edge cases outside the calculator.
