FAQ for Fourthwall creators

How much do I keep from a Fourthwall membership sale?

In this v1, memberships always carry a 5% Fourthwall platform fee. Your take-home then subtracts the editable payment-processing rate and fixed fee on top.

How does Free vs Pro change digital-product take-home?

That is the main plan difference modeled here: Free keeps the 5% platform fee for digital products, while Pro removes it. The calculator shows the exact dollar delta at your own price and volume.

If I mostly receive donations, what actually costs me money?

Donation mode keeps the Fourthwall platform fee at 0%, so the result isolates payment-processing drag. This is useful when your real question is “what do card fees actually take out of tips?”

Why is merch missing?

Because merch/product-catalog margin math needs product cost, shipping, fulfillment, and sometimes variant-level assumptions. That is a separate problem, so this page keeps the branded wedge narrow and trustworthy.

This page is intentionally narrow: memberships, digital products, and donations only. That constraint is the point — it keeps the numbers transparent enough to trust quickly.
