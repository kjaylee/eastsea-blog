FAQ

How much does a fractional CMO cost?
Many engagements land somewhere between a few thousand and low five figures per month, but the real answer depends on senior hours, meeting load, channel complexity, expected ownership, and whether support work is bundled.
Should I quote hourly or monthly?
Hourly pricing is useful for scoping, but most fractional CMO relationships are sold as monthly retainers because the buyer is purchasing continuity, strategic context, and leadership—not just isolated tasks.
Should onboarding be billed separately?
Usually yes. Kickoff strategy, audit work, messaging alignment, and KPI setup create front-loaded effort. Separating onboarding protects the retainer from month-one margin compression.
