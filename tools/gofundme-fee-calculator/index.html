Copy-ready summary

Does GoFundMe charge a platform fee?

For US individual/business fundraisers, GoFundMe states there is no fee to start or manage a fundraiser. This tool therefore models platform fee as 0% in the default assumption set.

What changes in recurring monthly donation mode?

GoFundMe says recurring monthly donations carry an additional 5% fee per donation. In this v1 tool that fee is modeled as donor-side checkout lift rather than organizer-side revenue loss.

Why are the rates editable?

Because country, payment method, and product-specific conditions can vary. The goal is a conservative planning calculator, not a claim that every transaction will settle at identical terms.
