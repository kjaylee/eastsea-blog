Stop guessing with character counts. This checker estimates Google title pixel width and meta description pixel width across desktop and mobile budgets, then shows whether your snippet is safe, tight, or already over the line.

Pixel widths are estimates based on a weighted character model. They are designed for fast publishing QA, not for claiming exact Google rendering down to the last pixel.
