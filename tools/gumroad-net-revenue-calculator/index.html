Estimate take-home from direct/profile sales versus Discover sales with Gumroad’s public 10% + $0.50 direct fee, 30% Discover fee, optional direct-processing assumptions, and payout drag. 크리에이터가 실수령액을 빠르게 판단할 수 있도록 만든 정적 계산기입니다.

Gumroad is now a Merchant of Record, so this page treats tax handling as an informational note rather than a creator fee line. Direct card/PayPal processing is modeled as a user assumption because Gumroad’s public platform fee does not bundle that cost into the direct-sale fee.

Formula snapshot

  • Discover fee = 30% all-in on Discover gross sales
  • Payout drag = creator proceeds × annual cash-cost rate × delay days / 365
