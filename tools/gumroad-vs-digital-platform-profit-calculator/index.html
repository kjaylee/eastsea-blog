Frequently Asked Questions
Does Gumroad charge monthly fees?
Gumroad's free tier charges 10% per sale with no monthly fee. Their Pro plan ($10/mo) reduces the fee to 8% + payment processing. This calculator uses the free tier rates by default.
Which platform is best for selling digital templates?
For creators selling templates, presets, or digital assets with under 200 monthly sales, Gumroad or Lemon Squeezy typically win on net revenue due to no monthly fee. Above 200+ sales where the $29 Shopify/Stans fee breaks even, Shopify or Stan Store add value through store features.
What are Lemon Squeezy's extra fees?
Lemon Squeezy charges 5% per transaction plus payment processing (2.9% + $0.30). They also charge a 1-5% risk fee on some products. Their Lemon Squeezy Plus ($9/mo) removes the per-sale fee for most creators.
How does Stan Store compare to Gumroad?
Stan Store charges $29/month with 0% platform fee (you pay only Stripe processing at 2.9% + $0.30). Gumroad charges 10% per sale with no monthly fee. Stan Store wins if you sell enough volume that the $29/mo breaks even vs Gumroad's 10% cut.
