💡 What Is Impermanent Loss?
How AMM pools work: Automated Market Makers (like Uniswap) use the formula x × y = k. When token prices change, arbitrageurs rebalance your pool, leaving you with more of the cheaper token and less of the pricier one — always worth less than simply holding both at the new prices. The loss is "impermanent" because it disappears if prices return to the entry ratio.
