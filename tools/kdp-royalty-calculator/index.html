KDP Royalty Calculator

Estimate how much your Kindle Direct Publishing eBook can keep after VAT, 35% vs 70% royalty rules, delivery-cost drag, and Kindle Unlimited page reads.

How this models KDP

  • 35% plan pays a flat 35% of net list price.
  • 70% plan uses 70% minus delivery costs only in eligible territories.
  • Sales outside 70%-eligible territories fall back to 35%.
  • Kindle Unlimited earnings are modeled separately as pages read × payout per page.

Why this page matters

KDP authors usually need one fast answer: what does each sale actually pay me, and what monthly combination of sales plus KU pages gets me to my target? This page keeps that answer on one screen.
