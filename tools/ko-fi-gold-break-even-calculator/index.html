Formula and assumptions

Applicable revenue = memberships + shop sales + commissions

Free plan fee = applicable revenue × 5%

Gold cost = $12/month

Break-even threshold = $12 ÷ 0.05 = $240

This is intentionally a narrow upgrade-decision calculator, not a full payout estimator. It does not model taxes, regional payment fees, refunds, or historical Gold pricing.

FAQ

Do tips count toward Ko-fi Gold break-even?
No. Tips are excluded because Ko-fi already charges 0% service fee on tips, so Gold does not create a platform-fee advantage there.
Why are Stripe and PayPal fees separate?
Because those processing fees usually apply on both Ko-fi free and Gold paths. They matter for total payout, but they do not change the core Gold-vs-free threshold in this narrow decision tool.
What should I do if I am close to $240?
Treat this as a directional line. If your applicable revenue is hovering around $240, the choice is roughly neutral and you can decide based on non-fee features, convenience, or expected growth next month.
