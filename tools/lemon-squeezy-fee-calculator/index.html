Lemon Squeezy Fee Calculator

Estimate tax-inclusive Lemon Squeezy fees, payout drag, and the list price you need to net a target amount after payout.

Editable defaults model Lemon Squeezy public fee assumptions only. Taxes are collected from the customer and are not treated as creator revenue in this calculator.

Inputs

  • Base platform fee and fixed fee are editable assumptions so you can match your own Lemon Squeezy setup.
  • Fees are modeled on the tax-inclusive customer total, but taxes themselves are not counted as creator revenue.
  • International, PayPal, subscription, and payout-mode choices can materially change take-home.
  • Use the extra marketing fee field for abandoned-cart or affiliate-style fee drag when relevant.
