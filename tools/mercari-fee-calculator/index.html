FAQ / 설명

Why no Buyer Protection fee here?

Mercari lists Buyer Protection as a buyer-side fee. This calculator focuses on seller proceeds and seller-controlled pricing.

Why is shipping in the fee base?

Mercari’s public help text says the 10% seller fee applies to item price plus buyer-paid shipping, so shipping collected still creates fee drag.

How should I handle Instant Pay?

If one sale effectively absorbs the whole transfer, enter $3. If you batch multiple sales into one payout, enter your allocated per-sale amount instead.
