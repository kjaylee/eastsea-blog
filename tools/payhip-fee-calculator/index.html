Payhip Fee Calculator

Estimate creator take-home under Payhip Free, Plus, and Pro using public plan pricing, refunds, and processor assumptions. Compare the 5%, $29 + 2%, and $99 + 0% structures, then see the price required to hit your target monthly net profit. 페이힙 플랜 업그레이드 전에 실제 순이익을 빠르게 확인하는 정적 계산기입니다.

Payhip plan fees and payment-processor fees are modeled separately. Official Payhip help-center pricing says Free = 5%, Plus = $29/month + 2%, and Pro = $99/month + 0%. Stripe/PayPal fees are user planning assumptions, not Payhip fees.

Inputs · 입력값

PayPal fixed fees vary by currency. The built-in PayPal preset is a USD planning baseline only.
Enter valid inputs to calculate Payhip take-home.
Formula snapshot
  • Gross sales = average order value × monthly orders
  • Payhip plan fees = gross × plan rate + monthly plan fee
  • Processor fees = gross × processor rate + (orders × fixed fee)
  • Upgrade thresholds are plan-only break-even gross sales: Free→Plus, Free→Pro, Plus→Pro
