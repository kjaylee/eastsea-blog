Assumptions & scope
  • Printify pricing varies by product and provider, so this calculator keeps product cost and shipping editable instead of pretending there is one universal baseline.
  • Premium savings are modeled only on product cost, with the discount input exposed because official pricing says discounts are up to 33%.
  • Storefront fee % and fixed fee are editable assumptions so the same page works as a Printify pricing calculator for Etsy, Shopify, or a direct-store flow.
