How to Use This Calculator

  • Cap Rate — Net Operating Income divided by purchase price. Higher = better return. Typical range: 4–10%.
  • Cash-on-Cash Return — Annual cash flow divided by your total cash invested. Measures return on your actual out-of-pocket money.
  • 1% Rule — Quick screening: monthly rent should be ≥ 1% of purchase price for a potentially good deal.
  • Vacancy Rate — Expect 5–10% vacancy in most markets. Adjust based on your local area.
  • Maintenance — Budget 5–10% of gross rent. Older properties may need more.
  • Property Management — Typically 8–12% of rent if using a manager. Set to 0% if self-managing.
