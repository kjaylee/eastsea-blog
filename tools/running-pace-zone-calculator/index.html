💡 How to Use Your Zones
80/20 Rule (Polarized Training): Elite runners spend ~80% of training time in Zones 1–2 (easy, conversational) and only ~20% in Zones 4–5 (hard, uncomfortable). Avoid the "grey zone" (Zone 3) — it's too hard to count as recovery but too easy for major aerobic gains. Most recreational runners overtrain in Zone 3 and under-train in Zone 1.
Heart rate zones are estimates. Consult a sports physician before beginning intense training programs.
