FAQ

Why is the payout-per-stream field editable?

Spotify explicitly says there is no fixed per-stream payout. Final royalties depend on streamshare economics, territory mix, subscription mix, and rights-holder contracts. This tool treats payout per stream as a scenario input so the result stays honest.

What does “artist share” mean here?

It represents the share that reaches you from the royalty pool before distributor fees and collaborator payouts. If you only keep 70% of the master-side revenue under your deal, enter 70.

Why does the tool show a 1,000 annual-stream gap?

Spotify’s Loud & Clear FAQ explains that recording royalties are handled differently for tracks below 1,000 streams in the previous 12 months. This helper turns your monthly pace into an annual estimate so you can see the remaining gap quickly.
