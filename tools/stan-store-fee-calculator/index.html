Copyable summary

Why compare annual and monthly?

Stan’s public annual pricing lowers the monthly-equivalent fee. This calculator treats annual plans as monthly equivalents so the take-home comparison stays apples-to-apples.

What does “Creator Pro uplift needed” mean?

It is the extra monthly gross sales Creator Pro would need to produce, at your current margin mix, just to cover its higher subscription fee versus Creator.

What if my processor fees differ?

Switch to the custom preset and override the base rate, flat fee, international add-on, and recurring-fee add-on with your actual mix.
