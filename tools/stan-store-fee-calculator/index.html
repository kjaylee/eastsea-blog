Stan Store Fee Calculator

Estimate what a Stan creator actually keeps after Creator vs Creator Pro plan fees, US Stripe standard / Afterpay / Klarna fees, payout drag, refunds, and fixed monthly costs.

Stan’s transaction fee is modeled as 0%. This v1 focuses on US Stripe-based sales only and excludes PayPal. Use it as a planning model, not a billing statement.

Assumptions & interpretation
  • Stan transaction fee is modeled as 0%, but plan cost still applies: Creator $29/mo or Creator Pro $99/mo.
  • US Stripe presets are modeled from public Stan help docs: standard 2.9% + $0.30, Afterpay 6.0% + $0.30, Klarna 5.99% + $0.30.
  • Payout fees are charged per payout event at 0.25% + $0.25 with a $1 minimum, so payout cadence matters.
  • PayPal and non-US country presets are intentionally excluded from this v1 slice.
