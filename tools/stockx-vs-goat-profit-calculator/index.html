StockX vs GOAT Profit Calculator

Compare StockX and GOAT seller fees, payout, net profit, and match-price deltas for the same resale scenario before you decide where to list.

This v1 reuses the shipped public-baseline models already inside EastSea: StockX seller-level fees + 3% processing + $5 US minimum seller-fee interpretation, and GOAT public baseline 9.5% commission + $5 seller fee (or your custom override) plus optional refund-planning loss.

Scenario inputs

Enter a scenario to compare both platforms.
  • StockX output uses the shipped seller-level baseline and reports StockX payout after fees.
  • GOAT output uses the shipped baseline or your custom override and reports GOAT payout before seller costs.
  • The actual winner is decided by final net profit after all seller-side costs.
  • Taxes, FX, special regional programs, and non-public penalty rules are outside this v1.
