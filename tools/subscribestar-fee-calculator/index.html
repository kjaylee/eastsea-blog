What this calculator is assuming

Why multiple processing presets?

SubscribeStar publishes a fee range rather than one universal processing fee. The presets help you compare average, high-ticket, floor, ceiling, and subscriber-paid scenarios without pretending one number fits everyone.

What is cash available now?

It is your modeled post-fee, post-reserve, post-payout, post-ops cash for the current month. If reserve is enabled, the held amount is excluded from cash now but still shown separately.

How is break-even estimated?

The model keeps your current average charge amount constant as revenue scales. If your tier mix changes materially, treat the target outputs as directional rather than exact.
