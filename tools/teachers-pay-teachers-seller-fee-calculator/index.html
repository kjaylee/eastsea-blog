FAQ

Why does Premium sometimes show a $0.15 fee?

TPT says Premium sellers pay a $0.15 transaction fee only on orders totaling less than $3. This tool uses your modeled sale price as a single-resource order baseline.

Why are there two Premium break-even numbers?

A new seller compares Premium against starting with Basic, so the incremental cost is $30.95. An upgrade scenario treats the earlier $29 Basic fee as sunk, so the Premium payback uses the full $59.95 annual fee.

Does this include taxes or cart complexity?

No. This is a planning model for payout math, not a tax calculator or a full multi-item cart simulator.
