Frequently Asked Questions
How does Telegram Stars monetization work?
Telegram Stars are purchased by users and spent on Mini Apps, games, and bot services. Developers receive ~70% of the Stars face value (1 Star ≈ $0.01 USD face value, developer gets ~$0.007). Stars can also come from in-app purchases within your Mini App.
What is a realistic Stars conversion rate for a Mini App?
Industry benchmarks vary wildly by category. Games typically see 2-5% conversion (users who spend Stars). Utilities and productivity apps see 0.5-2%. Highly engaging games with energy systems and premium features see up to 8-10%. Start conservative with 2%.
Can I monetize a Telegram Mini App with advertising?
Yes, Telegram opened Ads revenue for Mini Apps in 2024. eCPM rates range from $1-5 for most categories, with gaming and finance apps commanding higher rates. Ad fill rate depends on your user's geography and session length.
What's the minimum viable MAU for Telegram Mini App revenue?
With 1,000 MAU and 2% Stars conversion, you'd earn ~$0.70/day or ~$21/month from Stars alone. At 10,000 MAU, realistic monthly revenue is $200-500 from Stars + Ads combined. Most developers consider 50K+ MAU for meaningful income.
