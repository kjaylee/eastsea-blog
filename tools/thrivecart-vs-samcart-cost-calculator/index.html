Formula snapshot
Monthly contribution margin = gross − refunds − processor fees.
ThriveCart total platform cost = upfront fee + annual add-ons × months / 12.
SamCart total platform cost = (monthly fee + growth surcharge) × months.
SamCart required lift = extra monthly platform cost ÷ contribution margin rate.
Important scope note
ThriveCart’s default upfront fee here is an editable market-observed example, not a guaranteed live quote. SamCart’s public pricing clearly starts at $79/mo, but if your account pricing changes with revenue, model that using the growth-surcharge input rather than treating any hidden tier schedule as universal truth.
