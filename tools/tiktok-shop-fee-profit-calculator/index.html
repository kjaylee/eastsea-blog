What is TikTok Shop's standard referral fee?

TikTok Shop charges a referral fee on each completed order. The standard rate is 6% of the qualified fee base (customer payment + platform discount − tax). New sellers or promotional campaigns may qualify for a reduced 3% rate.

What is the refund administration fee?

When a buyer receives a refund, TikTok Shop may retain a refund admin fee equal to 20% of the referral fee on that order, capped at $5 per refunded order/SKU.

Does this calculator cover all TikTok Shop fees?

This is a single-SKU planning estimate. It models the referral fee and refund admin fee. Category-specific overrides, shipping charges to buyers, creator/affiliate commissions, and settlement timing are not included in this v1.

How should I use the "Custom" fee mode?

Select "Custom" if your category or agreement uses a referral rate different from the standard 6% or promo 3%. Enter your actual rate to get a more accurate estimate.
