📖 How TOTP Works

T = floor(UnixTime / Step) — The counter increments every step seconds (default 30).

HOTP(secret, T) — HMAC-SHA1 is applied to the counter, then truncated to get a numeric code.

RFC 6238 — The standard used by Google Authenticator, Authy, Microsoft Authenticator, and most 2FA apps.

⚠️ Never share your TOTP secret. Anyone with the secret can generate valid codes.
