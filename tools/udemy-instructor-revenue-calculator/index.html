FAQ / scope

Are 97%, 37%, and 25% guaranteed official payouts?

No. They are editable planning defaults based on commonly cited channel-share assumptions. Use this tool for scenario planning, not accounting or tax reporting.

Does this model Udemy Business revenue?

No. Udemy Business payouts are consumption-based and need a different model. This page focuses on premium course sales mix economics.

Why include refund drag separately?

Refunds hide the real margin. Modeling them explicitly prevents optimistic take-home estimates when course sales volume looks good on the surface.
