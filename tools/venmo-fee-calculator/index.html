Assumptions & interpretation

  • Personal goods & services fees are modeled at the official 2.99% rate.
  • Business profile fees use 1.9% + $0.10, while Tap to Pay uses 2.29% + $0.09.
  • Instant transfer uses the public 1.75% fee with a $0.25 minimum and $25 maximum per transfer.
  • Refunds are modeled conservatively as lost revenue without fee reversals.
