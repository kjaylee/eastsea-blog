FAQ

Is this a full Whop platform fee calculator?

No. This tool intentionally focuses on Whop Payments public fee primitives: payment processing and payout fees. It avoids pretending to model every possible platform-level commercial arrangement.

Why do payout fees depend on transactions per payout?

Because some payout methods charge a fixed amount per payout. If you withdraw after every sale, fixed payout drag is much harsher than if you batch 10 or 25 sales into one payout.

Why is “international local banks” excluded?

Whop Docs says it varies by country. That is not deterministic enough for a trustworthy calculator, so it is intentionally left out.
